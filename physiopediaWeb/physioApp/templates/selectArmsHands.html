Name of condition:Dupuytren's contracture
Finger will start to bend towards the palm and cannot be fully straightened,the most common factor contributing to this injury is due to genetics and 70% of the patients have past family history of the condition.

Name of condition:Broken finger
Fingers are easily broken or injured when engaging in daily activities.It occurs when the finger is bent forcefully in the wrong direction(such as sideways force) or collision with another person.

Name of condition:Trigger finger
Trigger finger occurs when the tendon in the finger or thumb thickens and gets caught when the finger is bent towards the palm, resulting in a painful clicking or locking.There is often no apparent cause of trigger finger,however is more common for women who have injured their finger before.

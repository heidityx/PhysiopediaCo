Name of condtion: Shin Splints
Normally occured in runners who run over a long distance on hard surfaces,other factors such as wrong posture and body alightment can contribute to the injury as well.

Name of condtion: Calf Strain
A calf strain may also be caused by gradual wear and tear associated with repetitive jumping and distance running or walking. For example: a sudden increase in speed from a fixed position or when lunging forward when playing tennis or squash can cause calf strain.

Name of condtion: Quad Pain
Quad pain is often caused by a direct blow to the muscle during sporting activities.If the quad muscles have torn (muscle strain) the site of pain is usually well localised in the front of the thigh.
